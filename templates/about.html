About

Writing is part of the class struggle

I'm an independent researcher, philosopher and activist. I write about topics like social revolution, the history of Marxism, feminism, tactics of class struggle and similar things. Not everybody is convinced that writing can contribute to class struggle. With this blog I intend to put this question to the test. There is a long intellectual tradition of people who shared the deep conviction that writing and reflecting on all kind of struggles and their interconnectedness can not only support these struggles themselves but is an integral part of them. I will try to engage with some of the most valuable works and contributions of these comrades.
